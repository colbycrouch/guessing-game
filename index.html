About Me

I was born and raised in Seattle, Washington. My wife, Jessie and I have been married for seven going on eight years, we have a 5 year old son named Cooper who turns 6 in Octoboer right after he starts kindergarten. We just sold our house in Seattle, and bought one twice as big in Duvall, which is 30 minutes east of Seattle. I started cooking at a young age and always liked to please people which has taken me very far into the hospitality industry, however it is time for a change. I am now taking Code 201 from Code Fellows to augment my education and make it possible to change careers.

Most recently I have been employed by the University of Washington since 2012 in a variety of positions, finally landing with the working title Sous Chef or Manager of Program Operations. Before the UW i was with El Gaucho Bellevue for a year as the Private Dining and Catering Chef. My work home before El Gaucho was Tutta Bella as a pizziola and before that i spent a year at Wing Dome as the kitchen Manager Early in my career i spent 5 years at Casa D's Taqueria as the Lead Cook and eventually Assistant Manager.
